About

Biography

I'm an M.Sc. student in Computer Engineering at Sharif University of Technology and a Research Assistant at the RIML Lab, where I work on compositional visual generation, text-to-image diffusion models, and cross-modal reasoning.

My research focuses on teaching machines to combine visual elements in structured and creative ways — spanning generative modeling, evaluation, and semantic alignment across vision and language. I'm always open to connecting with others interested in machine learning, computer vision, and AI research.
